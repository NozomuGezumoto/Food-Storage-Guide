# Privacy Policy

**Food Storage Guide**  
Last updated: March 2025

## Overview

Food Storage Guide ("the App") is designed with privacy in mind. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **only on your device** (local storage):

- **Purchase records**: Food items you register (name, storage location, expiry date, optional memo, optional photo)
- **Settings**: Notification time preference
- **App preferences**: Language and other in-app settings

**No data is sent to external servers.** All stored data remains on your device.

### Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|------------|---------|----------|
| **Notifications** | To remind you before and on the expiry date of registered items | Optional (you can decline; the App still works) |
| **Photo Library / Camera** | To attach photos to purchase records (e.g., receipt or product) | Optional |
| **Local Storage** | To save your purchase list and settings | Required for core functionality |

## Data Use

- **Purchase data**: Used solely to display your list, calculate remaining days, and schedule notifications.
- **Photos**: Stored locally with purchase records if you choose to add them. They are not uploaded anywhere.
- **Notifications**: Scheduled on your device to remind you of upcoming expiry dates.

## Data Sharing

**The App does not share your data with third parties.** No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
- When you mark an item as "Ate it," it moves to the History section but remains stored locally.

## Security

- Data is stored using the device's standard secure storage (e.g., AsyncStorage on iOS/Android).
- No data is transmitted over the network except when you explicitly use features that require it (e.g., opening external links).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is intended for general use.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
